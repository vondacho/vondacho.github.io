Edgelab – Risk engine API

April 2020 – March 2022

Why

Edge Laboratories SA wants to promote the public risk engine API as one of its main commercial products to be integrated by investment businesses.

What

Full-feature engineering and ownership in the scope of the risk engine API.

Requirements

  • To expose daily computed risk metrics related to asset portfolios with constant response time.
  • Every request must be treated in less than 100 milliseconds, independently of portfolio size.

Challenges

  • Daily challenges are availability and scalability in the context of 1 million of risk estimation requests on asset portfolios a day.
  • Daily ingestion of 1-2 millions of instruments from financial markets.
  • Daily pricing of 1-2 millions of instruments using mathematical risk models.
