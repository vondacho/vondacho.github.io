Hello, Bonjour, Hola, Guten Tag, Ciao,

I am Olivier von Dach, born 22.06.1972 in Switzerland where I am living and working.

I share my personal life with my wife. Besides working, we love doing sports outdoors and indoors, wellness, reading or studying and of course having some time with friends.

For many years I simultaneously dedicated myself to basketball and to my professional career. After having obtained a master of science in Information Technology at the University of Neuchâtel Switzerland, I had the opportunity to work in the passionate field of Software Development until today. Thanks to my sports and professional experience, I am convinced that producing good and complex software solutions is only possible by doing it in a team.

I have been working as a Software Engineer for 20 years now. I am a Software Architect, a Lead Developer and a Software Developer. These last 15 years my work was focused on the design and the development of enterprise-wide software solutions based on Java and web technologies. Today my drivers are large-scaled problems based on Functional and Reactive programming, Cloud computing, BigData, Microservices and to deal effectively with quality and continuous delivery in such ecosystems.

I am interested in working for an innovative IT company where I can bring an added value and where I can practice my knowledge, mainly software architecture and software development, and improve my soft and hard skills. I enjoy to work with motivated and open people.

Best regards.
